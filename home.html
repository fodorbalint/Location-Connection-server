Location Connection
For professional singles
Location Connection is a free dating app for anyone who doesn't compromise on functionality.

Key features:
Find people even without registration
It is needed for interaction only.
Choose from people who liked you
You can filter people by relation; that is:
  • everyone
  • people you neither liked or hid
  • those you liked, you matched with, added as friend or hid
  • users who liked or hid you
When a like is mutual, you can chat.
Search everywhere
You can narrow the list of people by using location and setting a maximum distance. The center point can be either your location, or an address you set.
Users are also searchable by their name, username, or keywords in their description.
Simple registration
You need to provide a picture, a name and a bio.
It is not required to specify age, height, weight or other things, but if someone does mention it in their bios, you can find them using number range search.
Be in control of your visibility
Your last active date, response rate or register date will determine if you appear on the top of the lists. The sort order can be reversed though, so watch out for your statistics.
Share your location
Make your location public, or share it only with people you liked, you matched with, or added as friend. The same goes with distance.
If you do not use own location, your will still show up for others if they do not filter the results by distance.
Get feedback on the messages you send
You can see if your messages were read and when.
Except for situations where someone sees your message in a background notification, messages will be marked seen or read depending on where your partner saw them.
Send location updates
This feature is available between matches. If you initiate location updates, your partner will see your location change on a map every 15-60 seconds depending on your settings.
Free, ad-free, and open-source
This app is created by a guy who did not find a girlfriend on traditional dating apps, it is therefore not used for monetary gains.
While maintaining an app store presence and running the server does cost money, it is on me for the time being, and should it exceed my budget, a donation page will be created.
You also deserve full transparency when using the app. If you are ever in doubt about a function and somewhat familiar with programming languages, take a look at the source code.
