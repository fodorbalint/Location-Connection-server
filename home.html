Location Connection
For professional singles
You can see who liked or hid you.
When a like is mutual, you can chat.
Registration made simple
You need to provide a picture, a name and a bio.
It is not required to specify age, height, weight or other things.
Instead, search is available for you to find people who wrote about these in their bios.
Filter by relation and geography
See all people, or those who:
  • you neither liked or hid
  • you liked
  • liked you
  • you matched with
  • you added as friend
  • you hid
  • hid you
For narrowing the list down by location, you can set a maximum distance, and use your own location as the center point, or set an address.
Location sharing and privacy levels
You can choose to make your location public, or share it only with people you liked, you matched with, or added as friend.
The same goes with distance.
If you don't use own location, your account is still fully functional.
Instant messaging with read receipts
Basic chat function, but you will also see whether your match has read your message and when, or if you wrote several lines, messages will be marked either seen or read depending on where they appeared for your match.
There is just one exception to this system: messages appearing in a notification.
Real-time location updates
There can be situations where it is hard to find each other, or you simply want to track each other's location.
Instead of having to continuously refresh the search results, if someone started a session with you, you can open their profile page, and see the location change on map every 15-60 seconds depending on their choice. Available between matches/friends.
No registration only means no interaction
You can still find people, just cannot like them. All search and filtering options are available, and if you turned on location, your timeline is accessible.
Free, ad-free and open-source.
The operating costs are currently about 10 USD per month, and paid by Google's introductory credit until September 2020.
It is estimated that hundreds or thousands of people can use the app without significant cost increase. Beyond that I may need donations to maintain the level of service, but no payments will be required from anyone.
