Location Connection
For professional singles
Location Connection is a free dating app for anyone who doesn't compromise on functionality.

Here you can:
Find people even without registration
It is needed for interaction only.
See who liked or hid you
When a like is mutual, you can chat.
Register without unnecessary details
You need to provide a picture, a name and a bio.
It is not required to specify age, height, weight or other things.
Instead, search is available for you to find people who wrote about these in their bios.
Filter by relation and geography
See all people, or those who you neither liked or hid, you liked, liked you, you matched with, you added as friend, you hid or hid you.
For narrowing the list down by location, you can set a distance, and use your own location as the start point, or set an address.
Share your location
Make your location public, or share it only with people you liked, you matched with, or added as friend. The same goes with distance.
If you don't use own location, your account is still fully functional.
See if your messages were read, and when
Except for situations where someone sees your message in a background notification, messages will be marked seen or read depending on where your partner saw them.
Send location updates
There can be situations where it is hard to find each other, or you simply want to track your partner's location.
Without having to continuously refresh the search results, you can follow them on the map as their position changes every 15-60 seconds. Available between matches/friends.
No ads are shown in the app, nor will you be required to pay. The costs are on me, and should they exceed my budget, a donation page will be created.
